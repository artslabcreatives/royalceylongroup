Who we are

As a newly established Ceylon Tea exporter we are eager to establish our name in the global market as a premier supplier of pure Ceylon black tea. We are a deep routed Plantation Company established early in the years of 1980 counting over thirty years in the Industry.

We are passionately indulged in producing the prime aromatic, flavorful, distinct Ceylon Tea; with a blend of traditional and modern technology bringing out the best in quality. A product that is too good for keepsake, hence expanding our services in distributing and exporting.

Quality has been the priority and driving force of our success; combined with the utmost passion and love for the product itself. We nurture the whole process from the sapling at nursery to the final grain of produced tea.
