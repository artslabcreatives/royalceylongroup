We are Royal Ceylon Leaf

Royal Ceylon Leaf is all about Quality Ceylon Tea with a blend of heritage

As a newly established Ceylon Tea exporter we are eager to establish our name in the global market as a premier supplier of pure Ceylon black tea. We are a deep routed Plantation Company established early in the years of 1980 counting over thirty years in the Industry.
